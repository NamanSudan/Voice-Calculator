This is a simple GUI based voice calculator which can perform various calculations such as:-

>>> addition, subtraction,division,multiplication,factorial,power,log,square root,trigo fucntions

Example1:- add 1 plus 2 plus 3 plus 4 plus 5 

Example2:- multiply 2 into 3 into 4

Example3:- log of 1000 base 10

Example4:- 2 raised 10

Example5:- sin90 #Takes angle in degrees

For more commands check out the coding!

U.I includes Dark and Light themes with Listening... animation

Requires an active internet connection for speech_recognition to work
